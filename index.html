FAQ

Questions

What trades does it support?▼

We support low-voltage/telecom, security and access control, fire alarm, electrical (Division 26), audio visual and paging, and HVAC/plumbing. If your trade isn't listed, contact us -- we can add custom trade support.

How accurate are the AI estimates?▼

The AI reads device schedules first (most accurate), then verifies with symbol counting. When both sources agree, accuracy is 95%+. You can always adjust counts before sending the quote.

What if blueprints aren't available?▼

The system can estimate from project description, building type, and square footage using industry standards. These are clearly marked so you know the confidence level.

Can I use my own pricing?▼

Yes. You set your vendor prices, labor rates, overhead, profit margins, and tax rate. The system uses your numbers for every quote.

Does it work with PlanHub and ConstructConnect?▼

Yes. The system auto-monitors both platforms for new bid invitations. New projects appear in your dashboard automatically with plans downloaded and analyzed.

How does the spec scanner work?▼

AI reads the project specification document, finds the Division 27 (or your trade's) sections, and extracts approved manufacturers, cable types, colors, and product requirements. The system then auto-selects the right vendor parts for the quote.

Can I cancel anytime?▼

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel anytime. No contracts, no setup fees, no hidden charges.

How long does setup take?▼

Most customers are generating quotes within 24 hours. We set up your vendor pricing, connect your PlanHub account, and configure your quote template. You start bidding the same day.
