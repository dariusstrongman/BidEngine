The Bid Mistakes BidEngine Doesn’t Make

HVAC bids get won when you size the equipment right and lose when the sheet metal goes long. BidEngine reads the mechanical equipment schedule for tonnage and CFM, takes off the duct sizes from the plan with the actual run lengths, and reconciles the diffuser count against the layout so you don't show up to install with 12 fewer diffusers than the field needs.
