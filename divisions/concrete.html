Why for Concrete

The Bid Mistakes BidEngine Doesn’t Make

Concrete bids hinge on three numbers your competitors get wrong: cubic yards (which depends on slab thickness, footing depth, and waste factor), rebar pounds by bar size, and the realistic pump hours for the pour schedule. BidEngine's concrete engine reads thickness off the structural notes, applies your local waste factor, and pulls rebar schedules from the typical details — so the takeoff that used to take a half-day is done before lunch.
